More about Them

The mountain gorillas are one of the two subspecies of the eastern gorilla.

There are only two population worldwide

As of June 2018, there were more than 1,000 individuals.

The most visited park is in the northwestern part of Rwanda

The other park is found in Uganda.
It is called Bwindi Impenetrable National Park.
